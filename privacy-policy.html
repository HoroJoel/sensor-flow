Privacy Policy - Sensor Data Recorder

Last Updated: July 12, 2025

Introduction

Joel Aravoena ("we", "our", or "us") operates the Sensor Data Recorder mobile application (the "Service").

This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal data when you use our Service and the choices you have associated with that data.

Information We Collect

Sensor Data

Our application accesses the following device sensors only when you choose to start a recording:

  • Accelerometer: To measure device acceleration
  • Gyroscope: To measure device angular velocity
  • Magnetometer: To measure magnetic field
  • Barometer: To measure atmospheric pressure
  • GPS: To obtain location coordinates (only when enabled)

Data Storage

  • All sensor data is stored locally on your device
  • We do not automatically send any data to external servers
  • You have complete control over what data is recorded and how it is used

How We Use Your Information

The collected data is used to:

  • Provide the core sensor recording functionality
  • Enable data export in useful formats (CSV, JSON)
  • Facilitate data analysis by the user

Data Sharing

Voluntary Export

  • You may choose to export data to external services configured by you
  • This export is completely voluntary and user-controlled
  • We do not share data with third parties without your explicit consent

We Do Not Sell Data

We never sell, trade, or transfer your personal data to third parties.

Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your data:

  • Secure local storage on your device
  • Data encryption during transmission (when applicable)
  • Data access only when the application is in use

Your Rights

  • Access: You can access all your data directly through the app
  • Export: You can export your data in multiple formats
  • Delete: You can delete your data at any time
  • Control: You control what sensors are recorded and when

Permissions

The app requests the following permissions:

  • Location: Only used for GPS sensor recording when enabled
  • Motion & Fitness: Used to access accelerometer, gyroscope, and related sensors

All permissions are used solely for sensor data recording purposes.

Data Retention

  • Data is retained locally on your device until you choose to delete it
  • No data is automatically transmitted to or stored on our servers
  • You can delete individual recordings or all data at any time

Children's Privacy

Our Service is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal data, please contact us.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.

International Users

This app complies with applicable privacy laws including GDPR (EU), CCPA (California), and other regional privacy regulations. All data processing is done locally on your device.
